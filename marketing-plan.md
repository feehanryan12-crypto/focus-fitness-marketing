# Focus Fitness Marketing Plan
**Location:** Levittown, PA
**Budget:** $200/month
**Target:** Adults 25–60 in Levittown who want to get stronger
**Brand Voice:** No bullshit. Efficient hard work. No gimmicks.

---

## Goal
Get more local people through the door for personal training and group classes by being the obvious choice for serious strength training in Levittown.

## Strategy

### 1. Facebook & Instagram Ads — $120/month
Your target (25–60, Levittown area) is very active on Facebook. This is your highest ROI channel.

**What to run:**
- Target zip codes: Levittown (19054, 19055, 19056, 19057, 19058)
- Age: 25–60
- Interests: weightlifting, fitness, strength training
- Ad types: short video clips of real training, before/after results, member testimonials

**Ad copy that fits your brand:**
> "No machines. No mirrors. No bullshit. Just real strength training in Levittown. First class free."

- Run 1-2 ads at a time, $4/day
- Rotate every 3–4 weeks and keep what works

---

### 2. Google Business Profile — Free
Set this up if you haven't. It's how people find you when they search "gym near me" or "personal trainer Levittown PA."

**Action items:**
- Claim/update your Google Business listing
- Add photos of the gym and training sessions
- Ask every new member to leave a Google review
- Post once a week (class schedule, tips, results)

---

### 3. Organic Social Media — Free
Post 3–4x per week on Facebook and Instagram. Keep it real — no stock photos.

**Content ideas:**
- Short clips of members lifting (with permission)
- "Tip of the week" — simple strength training advice
- Member spotlights
- Behind the scenes of a class or training session
- Straight talk posts: "Why cardio alone won't get you results"

**Tone:** Direct, honest, confident. Same voice as your brand.

---

### 4. Referral Program — Free
Word of mouth is your most powerful tool.

**Simple offer:**
> "Refer a friend who signs up — you both get one free month."

Tell every current member in person and post it on social.

---

### 5. Local Flyers — $35/month
Old school but works in tight-knit communities like Levittown.

**Where to post:**
- Laundromats, diners, barbershops, hardware stores
- Community bulletin boards
- Local Facebook groups (Levittown PA Buy/Sell, Levittown community groups)

**Flyer message:** Simple. Big headline: *"Get Strong. No Bullshit."* — gym name, phone, website, first class free offer.

---

## Lead Magnet
Offer a **free first class or free consultation** to lower the barrier to entry. Capture name + phone number. Follow up within 24 hours.

---

## Monthly Checklist
- [ ] Run and monitor Facebook/Instagram ads
- [ ] Post 3–4x per week on social
- [ ] Post once on Google Business
- [ ] Hand out or post flyers
- [ ] Follow up with all leads within 24 hours
- [ ] Ask new members for a Google review
- [ ] Check which ad is performing best, cut what isn't working

---

## 90-Day Goal
- 10 new members in first 90 days
- 20+ Google reviews
- 500+ local social media followers
